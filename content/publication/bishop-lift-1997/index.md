doi: 10.1098/rspa.1964.0005
abstract: A circular cylinder was placed in a flowing fluid with its axis across the
  stream. The fluctuating lift and drag forces, and the steady drag force were measured.
  The results for the stationary cylinder were given in a previous paper (Bishop &
  Hassan 1964). Here, the results are described and summarized for a cylinder that
  is made to oscillate transversely in a direction perpendicular to the stream.
