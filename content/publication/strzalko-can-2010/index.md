abstract: We consider the dynamics of the three-dimensional model of the die which
  can bounce with dissipation on the table. It is shown that for the realistic values
  of the initial energy the probabilities of the die landing on the face which is
  the lowest one at the beginning is larger than the probabilities of landing on any
  other face.
