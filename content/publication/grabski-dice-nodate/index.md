abstract: "In the paper some aspects of die throw dynamics are presented. Free fall
  of die as well as their collisions with table are analysed. Two models of collision
  are compared: first based on Newton's hypothesis and the second on Poisson's hypothesis.
  It is shown that from the point of view of dynamical systems dynamics of die is
  predictable."
