abstract: The stability of a vertical jet of water has been examined by imposing audio
  frequency disturbances. Flash photographs allow a direct measurement of the growth
  rate of the disturbances and the complete dispersion curve has been investigated.
  The effects of viscosity on the stability have been examined with glycerine-water
  solutions and have been found to be in good agreement with Chandrasekhar’s calculations.
