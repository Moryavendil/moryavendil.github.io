  have been investigated. This includes both the formulation of the problem with the

  may exist, either sinuous or varicose. Depending on the parameters of the problem

  modes may be unstable. These modes were mapped,in the parameter range of the present
  experiments and the sinuous modes were found to be the most unstable. Hot wire anemometry,measurements

  the waves reach a certain amplitude they break up which creates strong streaky structures
  in the jet.
